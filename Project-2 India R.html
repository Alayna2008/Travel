Kerala

Now to be honest the reason I chose to tell about Kerala out of all of the other states in India is because Kerala is my homeland . But honestly speaking Kerela is a great place with a few flaws of course but its definitely beautiful and a place you must visit whenever you travel to India.

Transport

I have many relatives at Kerala, so I never really needed to choose any mode of transport. But if you are a person who has never been to Kerala then its best if you choose to rent a car with a driver. I mean there are many modes of transport in Kerala but the cleanest and most preferable mode of transport for a tourist is renting a car with a driver.

Food

Keralites prefer food with lots of spices.So if you go out to try any restraunt in Kerala take a bottle of water .The most eaten food in Kerala is rice.Even breakfast meals are a form of rice and you will always need to buy a curry along with them.Briyani is also a famous lunch food in Kerala and make sure you eat it at least once when you visit.If you are a person who loves spicy food then Keralian food is perfect for your taste.
