Los Angeles

Los Angeles is a trip in America I hoped to go since I was a child. Since my cousins and their parents lived in America, we went with them, and it was an experience I will never forget. For me it was a very enjoyable trip. Even though me and my cousins were exhausted at the end of the day we went to sleep with a smile on our faces.

Transport

Los Angeles was a big place and there was a lot of ground to cover so the best option is to rent a car. Though it might be a bit boring it’s better to rent a car while in Los Angeles.

San Francisco

Los Angeles wasn’t the only city I visited in California I had went to San Francisco too. The Golden Bridge of San Francisco was mesmerising and was extremely beautiful. The most appropriate time to see the golden gate bridge is either during sunrise or sunset. The sight I had seen is a sight I will never forget in my life.
